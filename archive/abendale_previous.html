<h1>Abhijit Bendale</h1>

<p align="justify">I received M.S and Ph.D. from <a href="http://uccs.edu">University of Colorado Colorado Springs</a> under the supervision of <a href="http://vast.uccs.edu/~tboult/">Prof. Terrance Boult</a>.</p>

<p align="justify"> I was a researcher in <a href="http://cameraculture.media.mit.edu/"> Camera Culture Group </a> in <a href="http://www.media.mit.edu/">MIT Media Lab </a> (2010-2011) and <a href="http://web.mit.edu/mcgovern/">McGovern Institute of Brain Research </a> in <a href="http://www.mit.edu/">MIT </a> (2010-2011). I completed my undergraduate degree in Electronics and Telecommunications from <a href="http://www.unipune.ernet.in">University of Pune</a>. I spent Oct 2013 - Jan 2014 in beautiful Swiss Alps where I collaborated with <a href="http://www.idiap.ch/~marcel/"> S&eacutebastian Marcel </a>at <a href="http://www.idiap.ch"> Idiap Research Institute</a>. </p>


<p align="justify"> I defended my PhD in Fall 2015. Following PhD, I have joined <a href="http://sra.samsung.com"> Samsung Research America (SRA) </a> in Mountain View, CA. At SRA, I am part of <a href="http://thinktankteam.info">Think Tank Team</a>. </div>
