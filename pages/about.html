About Me

Hi, I'm Abhijit.

I'm an AI and Machine Learning executive with over 15 years of experience building and scaling production AI systems across computer vision, generative AI, multilingual large language models (LLMs), augmented and mixed reality, and real-time voice systems.

Most recently, I was Founder and CTO of Two AI, where we raised $20M in seed funding from Jio Platforms and Naver. I architected SUTRA, a dual-transformer multilingual LLM platform, and led products such as Zappy (20M+ messages) and AutoCameo (300K+ users), collectively generating $10M+ ARR. Our work included a national-scale deployment of AI-powered search and understanding on Jio AI Cloud, serving 400M+ users across multimodal inputs on CPU- and edge-efficient infrastructure.

Previously, I served as CTO of Samsung STAR Labs, an internal moonshot initiative, where I led a $30M+ R&D organization and the development of NEON, a real-time generative digital-human platform launched at CES 2020. The technology was later acquired and integrated into Samsung Electronics at a $50M+ internal valuation. Earlier at Samsung Research America, I helped architect Bixby Vision and on-device AI platforms deployed across 100M+ Samsung Galaxy devices, and led AR and mixed-reality platform initiatives in collaboration with teams at Microsoft and Google.

My academic background is in computer vision and machine learning. I received my M.S. and Ph.D. from the University of Colorado Colorado Springs under the supervision of Prof. Terrance Boult. During my Ph.D., I conducted research at the MIT Media Lab, the MIT McGovern Institute for Brain Research, and the Idiap Research Institute in Switzerland, contributing to vision and learning systems under DARPA- and NSF-funded projects. I'm an inventor on 30+ patents and have published in venues such as CVPR, but what I enjoy most is turning complex AI ideas into systems that actually ship and scale.

Across roles, I enjoy working close to the systems themselves. This includes designing architectures, reviewing models, debugging failures, and building and leading teams. Many people I have mentored have gone on to impactful roles at companies such as Microsoft, Google, Apple, OpenAI, Meta, and others.

In my free time, I enjoy outdoor activities like mountain biking, hiking, snorkeling, and snowboarding.
