Digital Humans and Generative AI

My patented research in digital / autonomous humans and generative AI pioneers the creation of hyper-realistic, multimodal avatars capable of perception, reasoning, and lifelike interaction. These systems combine speech, vision, gesture, expression, and cognition through advanced cross-modal learning and generative models, enabling digital humans to act as assistants, companions, educators, anchors, and immersive agents in real-world applications. Together, these patents establish a foundation for next-generation human–AI interaction, bridging deep generative intelligence with human-centric simulation for the metaverse, media, education, and enterprise engagement.

SLAM for AR/VR Applications

Patented research in event-based vision and SLAM (Simultaneous Localization and Mapping) advances the foundation of low-power, low-latency AR/VR systems. By integrating Dynamic Vision Sensors (DVS) with CMOS cameras and IMUs, these innovations address core challenges of tracking, mapping, depth estimation, and relocalization.
